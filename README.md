## 🖌 Overview  

Final Draft Professional Suite is the complete writing environment for screenwriters who need more than a word processor. It is a dedicated craft tool built around the specific demands of professional script writing.

Automatic formatting removes a whole category of friction from the writing process. Scene headings, character names, parentheticals, and transitions snap into place as you type, keeping your focus where it belongs — on the story.

Production features like scene numbering, revision tracking, and breakdown tagging make Final Draft Professional Suite useful not just for writers but for the entire production workflow downstream.

Index cards, the story map, and the navigator give you multiple perspectives on your script's structure, making it easy to identify pacing problems, develop subplots, and ensure every act hits its marks.

Final Draft Professional Suite is the writing tool that working professionals choose because it understands the craft of screenwriting from the first page to the last.
